Ingredients:

  • 1 medium-sized cauliflower
  • 1/2 cup maida flour
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Choped 2-3 green chilies
  • 2 medium-sized onion,
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chopped spring onions
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce or tomato ketchup

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the cauliflower: Coat cauliflower florets in a batter made of all-purpose flour, cornstarch, red chili powder, ginger-garlic paste, salt, and water. Deep fry until golden brown and crispy. Drain on paper towels.
  2. Make the Manchurian sauce: In a pan, heat oil and saut√© chopped garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Add chopped onions (and bell peppers if using) and cook until translucent. Stir in soy sauce, chili sauce or ketchup, vinegar, sugar, red chili powder, black pepper, and salt. Cook for a few minutes.
  3. Thicken the sauce: Add cornstarch slurry to the pan and stir until the sauce thickens.
  4. In a pan, cook pancetta until crispy.
  5. Combine and garnish: Toss the fried cauliflower in the sauce until well coated. Garnish with chopped spring onions.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy gobi.
